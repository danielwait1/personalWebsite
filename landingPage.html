About Me

Daniel is an Information Systems student at Brigham Young University, set to graduate in April 2026 with a GPA of 3.95. Currently a Software Consultant at the Missionary Training Center, he leads a team in developing efficient business solutions using tools like Microsoft Power Platform and SQL. Passionate about community service, Daniel has taught self-reliance courses to diverse families as a Volunteer Representative.

An Eagle Scout and National AP Scholar, Daniel balances his academic pursuits with his love for ultimate frisbee, hiking, and camping. He enjoys capturing the beauty of the outdoors through photography and following Formula 1 racing. With a commitment to leveraging technology for positive change, Daniel is excited to make an impact in the tech industry.
